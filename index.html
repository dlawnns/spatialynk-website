SpatiaLynk — Multi-Level & Explainable Location Recommendations

SpatiaLynk is a recommender application that helps you discover meaningful places by taking into your location and preferences, with clear explanations for your every recommendation.

SpatiaLynk allows you to easily get quick recommendations that you are bound to enjoy!

Key Features
Four things you'll love about SpatiaLynk.
Key Feature
Multi-Level Recommendations

SpatiaLynk adjusts recommendations based on zoom level: zooming out shows recommended districts or regions, while zooming in refines suggestions to specific attractions.

Key Feature
Explainable Recommendations

For each recommendation, SpatiaLynk generates a short explanation.
To increase trust and helps you understand why the we suggested a location.

Key Feature
Personalised Ranking

Candidate Recommendations are generated using advanced models based on user's distance, popularity, user interests, and past your interactions.

Premium Feature
Itinerary & Saved Collections

Premium users can generate day itineraries and save all recommendations into custom folders, improving your daily planning efficiency.

Who It's For

  • Visitors & Explorers: Discover new districts and hidden-gems fast.
  • Free Members: Get fuss-free personalised and explainable results using your preferences.
  • Premium Members: Unlock itinerary generation, saved collections, and priority ranking.
  • Business Partners: Highlight your business to our users and update your latest information.

Go Premium

More personal. More powerful. Still simple.

  • Smart Itineraries: Auto-build an itinerary for your entire day based on your preferences and time.
  • Saved Collections: Keep favourite places of interest in folders like “Weekend Spots”.
  • Priority Ranking: See better-matching places first as you explore.
  • Deeper Personalisation: Learns from feedback to refine every suggestion.

System Marketing & Value Proposition

Unique selling points, features, and user benefits

User Benefits

  • Time Savings: Reduces time spent searching for suitable places by combining filters, preferences, and location into a single system.
  • Higher Decision Confidence: Explanations help users trust the system and understand why certain locations are recommended.
  • Better Personalisation: Feedback (like/dislike) and search history improve future recommendations over time.
  • Context-Aware Suggestions: Recommendations consider where the user is and how they explore the map.

Target Users & Use Cases

  • Everyday Explorers: Students or young adults looking for cafes, study places, and hangout spots.
  • Visitors & Tourists: People exploring new areas who need location-aware recommendations with explanations.
  • Business Partners: Business Owners that want their businesses to appear in a smarter, more transparent recommendation platform.

SpatialLynk's Future Extensions

SpatiaLynk can be extended towards:

  • Smart city planning and urban mobility services.
  • Integration with tourism boards and local business networks.
  • More advanced natural-language explanations using NLG models.
  • Cross-platform deployment with deeper analytics for business partners and administrators.
